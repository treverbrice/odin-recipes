Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled Cheese is a go to comfort food, great for downer days and picky eaters

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread (white, sourdough, rye)
  • 4 slices cheese (american is best, but anything that melts good works)
  • 2 tablespoons butter or mayo
  • Optionally:
    • 2 cups spinach
    • 1/2 cup thin sliced onions
    • 1 cup scrambled eggs
    • 4 ounces meat (deli meats, diced bacon, diced ham)

Steps

  1. cover one side of both slices of bread in butter or mayo
  2. heat 10-inch skillet to medium high and place bread butter side down
  3. once browned, butter other side and flip
  4. place cheese on bread, ideally cover and wait till melted
  5. place any optional ingredients, make sure these are cooked first (especially the spinach)
  6. press the bread slices together and cut along the diagonal
  7. serve with tomato soup and a bit of green herb as a garnish
